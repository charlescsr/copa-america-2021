Day 6

Uruguay v Chile

Goals

Vargas 26'

Vidal(OG) 66'

An equal game, but you could say Uruguay had more chances and could’ve been more clinical. A few chances went over. Chile however took the lead when Vargas slotted it in from a tight angle. Uruguay equalised from Luis Suarez but the last touch was from Vidal so it went down as an own goal. Uruguay are yet to win in this tournament.

Argentina v Paraguay

Goals

Gomez 10’

Another 1-0 from Argentina which makes them the second team to qualify with Chile as well. Aguero missed an absolute sitter from an error in the Paraguay defense. Then Gomez did a brilliant dink to score what ultimately was the winner. Chances were there for both teams. Paraguay didn’t take their chances properly. Messi’s freekick was just wide and there was nothing much to talk about here.
