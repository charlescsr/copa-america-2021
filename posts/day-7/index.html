Brazil v Colombia

Goals

Diaz 10’

Firmino 78'
Casemiro 90'+10

Yes you are reading that right. There was 10 minutes of stoppage time and Brazil made a great escape. Colombia managed to score first from a great chance. It took Brazil till the second half and some missed chances to score when Firmino scored from a header from Lodi’s cross. Then after the ten minutes Casemiro scored a header from Neymar’s cross to keep the 100% record for Brazil and keep them at the top.
