Day 20

Colombia v Peru

Goals

Yotun 45'

Cuadrado 49'
Diaz 66'

Lapadula 82'

Diaz 90'+4

Good match from Colombia as they get third place. Great game from Diaz who scored a brace including an absolute screamer to win the game for them. The first half was slow with Peru having a lot of chances and finally scoring via Yotun before half time. The second half completely lit up with Cuadrado equalising with a great free kick in the gap between the wall. Diaz later put Colombia in front with a good goal. Lapadula later equalised for Peru but with almost the last chance of the game Diaz managed to score an absolute belter to win the game at the end.
