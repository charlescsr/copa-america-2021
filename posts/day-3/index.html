Brazil v Peru

Goals

Alex Sandro 12'
Neymar 68'
Everton 89'
Richarlison 90'+3

A brilliant performace from the “hosts” as they have almost sealed their passage to the next round. Neymar is actually 9 short of Pele’s record now. A brilliant cross from Jesus and Sandro scored the opener. Neymar later made it two with an amazing goal who also dived to try and win a penalty which was actually given before being turned around by VAR. Everton made it 3 from close range and Richarlison sealed the deal in injury time.
