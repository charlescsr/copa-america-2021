Day 8

Bolivia v Uruguay

Goals

Quinteros(OG) 40'
Cavani 79’

Finally Uruguay have a win and are through as well to the quarter finals. Bolivia had an early chance but missed it. Later finally, Uruguay managed to score when a cross was put into their own net by Quinteros who was trying to prevent the ball reaching Cavani. Later Cavani made it 2 when a low cross was met by him and he scored easily. Maxi Gomez could have made it 3 when a pass from Valverde left him with an open net and he shot wide. With the chances they got, it may have been 4 at least.

Chile v Paraguay

Goals

Samudio 33'
Almiron(P) 58’

Chile are stunned in this match. Most decisions didn’t go their way and they were not happy. Almiron finally had a good game for the national team when he assisted the first goal and scored from the penalty spot for the second. Paraguay and Chile are already through since the team in last place is Bolivia.
